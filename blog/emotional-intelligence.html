What is EQ

This is not my first time coming across the subject but this is my first time seeing this quote. It really captures the idea quite succinctly.

"The ability to monitor one's own and other's feelings and emotions, to discriminate among them and to use this information to guide one's thinking and actions." --- Salovey and Mayer 1990

In my own words emotional intelligence (EQ) is a measure of how aware someone is of both their own emotional state and that of those around them. It is about being able to read not just the room but individuals within the group. Distinguish nuances in someone's, including their own, emotional state from the actions, words, and body language. This leads to an understanding of when to listen and when to talk, it is a key pillar in a persons ability to operate within the world around them as we are social creatures and collaboration is one of our greatest strengths.

This differentiates from conventional intelligence (IQ). Which measures ones cognitive ability. IQ is an important part of being technically competant and so should not be neglected. Having a high IQ and being highly technically competant on it's own is not all that useful. There is a limit to what you can do alone and so collaboration and working with others is very important. If you want to solve truly complex and ground breaking problems you must work with others and this is where having a higher EQ really assists. It allows us to not only stretch our own personal limitations but also the limitations of those around us.

High EQ also helps us when we get stuck personally. Life can knock you down, if you haven't experienced this yet then you have only lived in the shelter. No shelter lasts forever and the march of entropy never stops. With a high EQ we are better able to identify internally what it is that we need to get back up again. Build a new shelter, adapt to the new shape of the world around us. Everything always changes and we must adapt or surrender to the grasp of entropy.

IQ makes a difference to the individual, EQ makes a difference to the group. IQ is like a computer on it's own, EQ is like the network protocols that allows those computers to communicate with each other effectively creating a combined ability/utility/power much higher than any one individual computer could be.
