Background

I have spent the last 7 years on my life on a personal growth journey after I failed my last tertiary course. I worry that too much focus on some of this assessment might have a negative effect on my own development as there are certain parts of my personality that I'm trying to improve.

For example below I have the Peacock personality result which labels me as not good with detail or time-management. Time-management has been a particular area of growth for me as I recognize it's utility and wish to be much better at it. I believe I have grown past "not good with time management" to a point where I am "capable of time management".

The detail line is interesting as well, because typically I have a high attention to detail. I might prefer to work on the big picture and make plans that have broad strokes but at the same time I'm very capable of narrowing in on the details.

I'm writing this mostly to remind myself that I'm not fixed into these categories and just because I prefer one way doesn't mean I shouldn't flex my ability to work the other way. In fact I think I should work hard to ensure I continue to do so for my continued growth.
